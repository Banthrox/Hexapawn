- [Tosh](https://twitter.com/__tosh)'s MVP guidelines
  - Here is a set of (totally arbitrary) MVP design constraints I sometimes recommend to founders / product managers to get a conversation going ("what if").
    - single player mode (valuable even if only 1 person uses it)
    - (almost) no manual data entry
    - (almost) no GUI (should fit on 1 smartphone screen max)
    - decision making process for adoption trivial (do I want this? yes)
    - adoption & onboarding without barriers (no need to learn anything, no need to sign up, no need to ask for permission, ‚Ä¶)
    - possible to build in 1 weekend
    - valuable enough that some people would be willing to pay USD 100 / month to use it
  - They are mainly for ideation (constraints are good) and (intentionally as well as unintentionally) would not be easy to satisfy by many conceivable products that also would be quite viable to pursue.
  - They aren't intended as _the_ set of design constraints for MVPs but rather optimised for reducing risk (which also often means reducing potential upside).
