      ## Focus

      Jan builds digital products, mainly web & mobile applications. Everything
      from concepts & product exploration, through UX, UI, visual & brand
      design, all the way to software development. He's done some [work in the
      past](/#work), and will probably do some more in the future.


      ## Design

      Jan designs in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe
      InDesign, Adobe Lightroom, and Adobe After Effects.


      ## Code

      Jan codes in Javascript: [React](https://github.com/facebook/react) &
      [React Native](https://github.com/facebook/react-native),
      [Redux](https://github.com/reactjs/redux), & [Apollo
      GraphQL](https://github.com/apollographql/apollo-client),
      [styled-components](https://github.com/styled-components/styled-components),
      [Node](https://github.com/nodejs/node) &
      [Express](https://github.com/expressjs/express). He usually edits that
      code in [VS Code](https://github.com/Microsoft/vscode), lints it with
      [ESLint](https://github.com/eslint/eslint) &
      [Prettier](https://github.com/prettier/prettier), and posts it on
      [Github](https://github.com).
