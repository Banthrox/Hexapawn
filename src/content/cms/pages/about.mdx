      ## Focus

      Jan builds digital products, mainly websites & web/mobile apps. Everything
      from concepts & exploration, through UX, UI, design, all the way to software development. 
      He's done [some work in the past](/#work), and is going to do some more in the future.

      ## Code

      Jan codes in Javascript: [React](https://github.com/facebook/react) &
      [React Native](https://github.com/facebook/react-native),
      [Apollo GraphQL](https://github.com/apollographql/apollo-client) & [Redux](https://github.com/reactjs/redux),
      [styled-components](https://github.com/styled-components/styled-components),
      [Node](https://github.com/nodejs/node) &
      [Express](https://github.com/expressjs/express). He usually edits that
      code in [VS Code](https://github.com/Microsoft/vscode), lints it with
      [ESLint](https://github.com/eslint/eslint) &
      [Prettier](https://github.com/prettier/prettier), and posts it on
      [Github](https://github.com/mrozilla).

      ## Design

      Jan prefers to design directly in code. However, when working with others, he's also proficient in Adobe tools, Sketch, etc.
